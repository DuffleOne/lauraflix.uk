Further setup

Content is hard to find

Annoyingly when you accept the invite to George'flix, PLEX puts it's own content suggestions at the top. This is all free to play unlicensed content, and is quite boring/dull.

You can fix this by following the steps below.

  • Visit Settings - Web/General in your browser.
  • Tap "Show Advanced" in the right of this panel.
  • Tap "Reset customization".
  • In this new setup guide, ensure George'flix is set as your default server.
  • Untick all pinned sources, select only the two items, "Movies" and "TV Shows".
Setting up a TV

If you have an Xbox, PlayStation, or Smart TV, you should be able to find the PLEX app through the app store of the device.

When you want to login you should open the app and it'll prompt you with a code such as PL9X. Enter this into the PLEX Link website.

Setting up a phone

Download the PLEX app from the PlayStore or AppStore of your device. Login with the same email you used to register initially.
